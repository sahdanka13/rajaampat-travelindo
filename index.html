TOP 1

Wayag Island

A tourist attraction in Raja Ampat that you shouldn't miss is Wayag. Wayag is an island consisting of a group of small islands filled with trees. Wayag Island is one of the icons in Raja Ampat and is a favorite of many tourists. You can take photos with a beautiful group of small islands in the background.

TOP 2

Misool Island

A tourist destination in Raja Ampat that will make your holiday even more complete is Misool. Misool is one of the largest islands in Raja Ampat with various interesting natural attractions. You can explore this island and visit every corner. Here you will find heaven on earth tucked away in the eastern part of Indonesia.

TOP 3

Arborek Island

Arborek is one of the tourist attractions in Raja Ampat which would be a shame if you missed it. This village is only an island with an area of 7 hectares, but the view is very beautiful. You only need 30 minutes to go around the village. During this exploration you will see how beautiful nature is in Raja Ampat. You can also play on the fine white sand here.
